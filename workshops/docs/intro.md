# ClimateStudio Workshops (Solemma Edition 2026)

This tutorial aims to provide a basic knowledge of using Climate Studio to conduct environmental performance analysis on digital twin building models. In the workplace, you will typically recieve a Rhino model from an architect and be asked to do simulation studies in order to facilitate iterative design choices on optimizing building energy efficiency, occupant comfort and sustainability.
